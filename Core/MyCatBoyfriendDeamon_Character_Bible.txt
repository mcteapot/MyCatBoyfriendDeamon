Character Bible
My Cat Boyfriend Is the Town Demon
________________


1. Hana Mori
Role: Heroine / POV character / curse-marked girl
Age: 18
Function in Story: Audience anchor, emotional heart, mystery driver
Personality
Hana is kind, stubborn, emotionally brave, curious, loyal, and secretly lonely. She is not passive. She gets scared, but she moves toward the truth anyway.
Visual Identity
* Japanese high school uniform
* red shrine bracelet from her missing father
* warm brown eyes
* soft dark hair
* faint blue cat-paw curse mark
Immediate Want
She wants to understand Kuro and prove he is not the monster everyone says he is.
Deeper Want
She wants to know what really happened to her missing father.
Fear
That she is only special because the curse chose her.
Flaw
She believes love means saving everyone, even when it destroys her.
Secret
She has seen blue flames since childhood but convinced herself they were dreams.
Power
After being marked, Hana can hear shadow spirits whisper secrets. Later, she can calm or command Kuro by speaking his hidden demon name.
Season Arc
Normal girl trying to survive → cursed girl investigating the town’s lies → heroine who chooses the supernatural truth → girl willing to break the town’s oldest curse.
Role in Plot
Hana drives the investigation, the romance, and the emotional stakes. Every major revelation should pass through her experience.
________________


2. Kuro Aonuma
Role: Male lead / cursed blue cat-demon guardian / school delinquent
Age: Appears 18
Function in Story: Forbidden romance, danger, mystery, supernatural protector
Personality
Cold, guarded, intense, lonely, protective, emotionally repressed.
Visual Identity
* black school uniform
* messy black hair
* glowing blue eyes
* hidden shrine chain
* broken Moonbell Collar
* blue cat-demon energy
Public Reputation
Students believe Kuro is violent, cursed, and responsible for accidents and disappearances.
Truth
Kuro has secretly protected the town from shadow spirits for years.
Immediate Want
Keep Hana alive without letting her get close.
Deeper Want
Be free of the collar curse.
Fear
That Hana will love him and unlock his final demon form.
Secret
He was not always a demon. He became cursed after making a pact to save someone.
Romantic Wound
The last girl who loved him helped seal him. Hana may be connected to that girl.
Season Arc
Isolated monster → reluctant protector → jealous lover → near-final demon → boy who chooses pain over possession.
Role in Plot
Kuro is the emotional and supernatural center of the story. His identity, memory, and curse form the main mystery.
Quark
Kuro is just a cat in human form; he doesn't speak other than meow. The entire cast can understand what Kuro is saying when me meows. Usually in a scene, Kuro will meow to communicate and the cast, mostly Hana will just agree and explain Kuro's positioning for the audience.  


Kuro will also have different meows spending his mood and that of the scene or other cat-like behavior. When in demon form, he only grows and roars like a lion or tiger.  


The only person that cant understand him of the cast is Ren. Ren sees that he is just a cat and wonders what everyone else is hearing when he can only hear mows coming from Kuro and cant understand what he is saying. 


Only until halfway through, when Ren and Kuro are able to work with each other do Ren finally have the ability to understand what Kuro is saying.


Continuity Rule
Whenever the arc says Kuro warns, says, argues, whispers, or explains something, interpret it as Hana reading his meows, growls, posture, claws, eye contact, tail and ear movement, and other catlike body language for the audience. If Hana is not conscious or not present, Kuro must communicate physically through urgent meows, snarls, gestures, blocking, pointing with claws, or trying to redirect attention. Ren cannot understand Kuro directly until their cooperation develops later, so Ren may misread Kuro when Hana is unavailable to translate.

3. Ren Shirogane
Role: Second male lead / exorcist heir / love triangle rival
Age: 18
Function in Story: Romantic rival, institutional pressure, elegant threat
Personality
Elegant, disciplined, protective, emotionally restrained, quietly jealous.
Visual Identity
* refined Japanese school uniform
* white prayer cord around wrist
* black violin case
* silver-gray eyes
* controlled posture
* polished, calm presence
Immediate Want
Remove Hana’s curse mark.
Deeper Want
Prove he is not like his ruthless father.
Fear
That Hana will choose Kuro and lose herself to the curse.
Secret
Ren’s family created the Moonbell Collar and has been using Kuro as the town’s disposable guardian.
Romantic Wound
Ren has loved Hana since childhood, but she forgot the night he saved her from the shrine.
Weapons
Ren’s weapon system is based on music and precision.
The Silver Requiem Violin
A sacred exorcist violin that releases silver spiritual energy through music.
Abilities:
* Binding Chord: freezes weaker spirits
* Silver Crescendo: sends blade-like sound waves
* Lullaby Seal: calms possessed humans
* Requiem Break: forbidden melody that damages high-level demons
The Silver Saber
Ren’s violin bow becomes a thin silver exorcist blade when charged with spirit energy.
Uses:
* close-range combat
* curse cutting
* spirit-chain severing
* defensive parries against claws and demon attacks
Combat Style
Ren fights with elegance, restraint, and ritual technique. He contrasts Kuro’s raw, emotional, feral combat.
Season Arc
Noble protector → jealous hunter → betrayer → ally who finally defies his family.
Role in Plot
Ren pressures Hana to choose safety. He also reveals the Spirit Warden world and forces Kuro into direct conflict.

4. Mina Park
Role: Hana’s best friend / gossip pipeline / emotional witness
Age: 18
Function in Story: School-world access, social media engine, comic relief with stakes
Personality
Bright, witty, loyal, observant, fashionable, chronically online.
Visual Identity
Mina should look like a believable young Japanese schoolgirl with subtle gyaru-inspired style.
Details:
* school uniform
* colorful nails
* bright accessories
* decorated phone
* tiny ghost-cat phone charm
* no school bag
* trendy but realistic styling
Immediate Want
Keep Hana from becoming social suicide.
Deeper Want
Be taken seriously, not just treated as comic relief.
Fear
Being left behind when Hana enters Kuro’s supernatural world.
Secret
Her older brother vanished near the shrine two years ago.
Season Arc
Funny best friend → jealous outsider → cursed victim → brave ally.
Role in Plot
Mina connects school gossip, social media, and supernatural rumors. Her investigations create clues and danger.

14. Mawari
Role: True town demon / main villain
Function in Story: Final antagonist, emotional manipulator, curse architect
Nature
Mawari is an ancient shadow spirit beneath the shrine. It feeds on secrets, shame, gossip, betrayal, jealousy, and forbidden love.
Visual Identity
* red eyes in reflective surfaces
* black catlike shadow
* too many tails
* distorted reflections
* whispering darkness
Public Lie
The town believes Kuro is the demon.
Truth
Kuro is the lock. Mawari is the real monster.
Immediate Want
Make Hana fall completely in love with Kuro before the ninth bell is purified.
Deeper Want
Use Hana and Kuro’s bond to unlock the shrine and possess Kuro’s body.
Fear
Honest love freely chosen.
Secret
Mawari created the myth that Kuro was evil.
Season Arc
Unseen whisper → rumor monster → human vessel → final body-snatcher.
Role in Plot
Mawari makes characters betray each other before it attacks them.

Character Relationship Web
Hana is pulled by:
* Kuro: forbidden truth and dangerous love
* Ren: safety and human future
* Mina: friendship and social reality
* Emi: family secrets
* Keiko: normal life
* Haru: missing father mystery
* Aoi: past-life destiny
* Mawari: emotional manipulation
Kuro is pressured by:
* Hana: hope
* Ren: execution threat
* Yoru: demon temptation
* Tama: guilt
* Aoi: betrayal memory
* Mawari: possession
Ren is pressured by:
* Hana: love
* Kuro: jealousy and moral conflict
* Daigo: family duty
* Spirit Wardens: obedience
* Silver Requiem Violin: inherited power
* Mawari: fear of losing control
Mina is pressured by:
* Hana’s secrets
* school gossip
* missing brother mystery
* fear of being left behind
* social media consequences
________________


Core Character Principle
Every major character should either:
* pull Hana toward Kuro
* pull Hana away from Kuro
* reveal a lie about Kuro
* expose a secret about Hana
* feed Mawari through betrayal, shame, or desire
No character should exist only for exposition.
