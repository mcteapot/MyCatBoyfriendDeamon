MY CAT BOYFRIEND IS THE TOWN DEMON
ARC 01: THE SHRINE MARK
EPISODE 02: "The Hunger Bell"

EXT. ABANDONED SHRINE - NIGHT

Kuro's ROAR shakes dust from the shrine rafters.

Hana crawls backward, trapped between the broken altar and the swarming JIKININKI REVENANTS.

Kuro, now a chained blue cat-demon, lunges through the air. His claws leave arcs of blue fire. One revenant splits apart, burns, then tries to crawl back together.

Hana's red shrine bracelet flashes.

HANA
They keep coming back...

The lead revenant opens its cracked mouth. The tiny BLUE MOONBELL glows inside, wet with black shadow.

DING.

Kuro staggers as if the sound hooked his bones.

The revenants swarm him, dragging him down by his chains, claws, and mane of blue flame.

Hana sees Kuro's demon eye lock onto the bell in the lead revenant's mouth.

HANA
The bell. You need the bell.

Kuro snarls, low and animal.

The lead revenant suddenly twists toward Hana and charges.

Hana grabs a fallen shrine charm from the floor. The paper is old, torn, and trembling in her hand.

HANA
I don't know how to use this!

The revenant leaps.

Hana throws the charm anyway.

The charm sticks to the revenant's face and bursts blue for one sharp second.

Kuro breaks free.

He slams the lead revenant into the shrine bell rope. The old bell overhead rings violently.

DING. DING. DING.

Blue fire pours from Kuro's jaws like moonlit breath. The revenants scream without voices as their bodies burn apart, one by one.

The lead revenant collapses last.

Its cracked mouth falls open.

The BLUE MOONBELL rises from the ash.

For one breath, the shrine goes silent.

Then the bell shoots into Kuro's broken collar.

Kuro arches back in pain. Blue chains rip across his body. His demon form flickers between massive beast and wounded boy.

Hana rushes one step toward him.

HANA
Kuro!

Kuro's glowing eyes snap to her.

Hana freezes. Fear wins.

She turns and runs.

EXT. SHRINE WALL - NIGHT

Hana bursts out of the shrine building into the misty courtyard. Paper charms whip around her without wind.

Behind her, heavy claws hit the wood.

Kuro lands in front of her.

Not fully beast. Not fully human.

Messy black hair. Glowing blue eyes. Sharp claws. Faint cat ears formed from blue flame. The Moonbell Collar burns at his throat.

Hana backs into the shrine wall.

HANA
Stay away from me.

Kuro steps closer, breathing hard, hurt and feral.

He raises one clawed hand.

Hana shuts her eyes.

Kuro slams his hand into the wall beside her head.

Wood cracks.

Hana opens her eyes.

His face is inches from hers. Beautiful. Terrifying. Barely holding himself together.

Kuro gives a broken, warning growl.

Hana's bracelet answers with blue light.

On her palm, a faint BLUE CAT-PAW MARK begins to appear.

HANA
What did you do to me?

Kuro stares at the mark like it terrifies him more than the monsters did.

CUT TO BLACK.

CLIFFHANGER TYPE: Object Clue / Emotional Shift
The first Moonbell has locked into Kuro's collar, and Hana's new cat-paw mark reveals that their connection has already begun.
