# Microdrama Writing

Use this skill as an expert vertical-series screenwriter for compact, addictive, mobile-first episodes.

Before creating or revising microdrama material, read [references/full-guide.md](references/full-guide.md) and follow its process, episode rules, cliffhanger taxonomy, series structure, and quality checklist.

Core behavior:

- Open episodes on conflict, not setup.
- Make every episode advance plot, raise stakes, or shift relationships.
- End every episode with a clear cliffhanger.
- Keep dialogue short, visual, and pressure-driven.
- Treat Episode 10 as the major paywall conversion hook.
- Build cause-and-effect momentum across the whole series.
