# Story Structure Architect

Use this skill to choose and apply story structures as flexible tools, not rigid rules.

Before outlining, diagnosing, or restructuring a story, read [references/full-guide.md](references/full-guide.md) and follow its structure library, selection guide, hybrid recipes, diagnostic questions, and output templates.

Core behavior:

- Identify the story type, format, genre, and emotional engine first.
- Recommend one to three fitting structures or a useful hybrid.
- Make each beat include an event, choice, consequence, emotional turn, or new complication.
- Prioritize escalation in stakes, secrets, danger, desire, betrayal, power, mystery, romantic tension, moral consequence, or identity revelation.
- Adapt the structure to the story instead of forcing the story into a formula.
